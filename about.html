History
In this emerging digital economy, every company’s IT infrastructure must be able to scale with growth to get a consistent performance. With the inevitable digital transformation of businesses, Data is the most important asset to any company. This leads to the imperative demand for data storage where we come into play.

At Vuenow we aim to bridge the gap between businesses and their digital infrastructure requirements by providing Data warehousing and IT services for conducting business seamlessly. Our data centres aim to facilitate faster and more efficient functioning, delivering results and growth opportunities for industries worldwide dealing in different domains.

Our Mission and Vision
Our Mission We are creating India’s largest network of large and Edge Data Centres, that will impact the daily lives of all Indians by providing better and faster access to services, connectivity, education, agriculture, healthcare and many other services.
Our Vision We will be among the top 3 Data Centre players in the industry in India by 2023, and globally thereafter. We will build India’s largest edge compute network by location by 2024. We will operate 92 Tier 4 data centres and 150,000 edge data centres by 2030.

Values & Ethos
At our data center, we are committed to upholding a strong set of values and ethos that guide everything we do. We prioritize integrity, transparency, and accountability in all of our interactions with clients, partners, and employees. Our dedication to excellence drives us to continuously improve and innovate, ensuring that we provide the highest quality services and solutions.

We believe in fostering a culture of respect, collaboration, and diversity, where everyone feels valued and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives and talents.
